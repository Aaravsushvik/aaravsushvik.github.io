Who Am I?

Hello! I am Gadiparthi Sai Sushvik — a student, technology enthusiast, and aspiring civil servant from India, committed to a path that connects innovation with public service.

My world exists at the intersection of two forces: technology and governance. I am equally fascinated by artificial intelligence and the architecture of policies that shape millions of lives. To me, these are closely connected — and I want to understand both deeply.

Living with Cerebral Palsy has taught me patience, adaptability, and persistence. It continues to shape how I approach learning and problem-solving. I move forward steadily — learning, building, and growing one step at a time.

My Vision

I want to serve as a civil servant who understands both technology and governance. I am especially interested in digital governance, accessibility, education systems, and how technology can improve public service delivery at scale.

Academic Path
