About Me

Who Am I?

Hello! I am Gadiparthi Sai Sushvik — a student, technology enthusiast, and aspiring civil servant from Hyderabad, India, committed to a path that connects innovation with public service.

My world exists at the intersection of two forces: technology and governance. I am equally fascinated by artificial intelligence and the architecture of policies that shape millions of lives. To me, these are closely connected — and I want to understand both deeply.

I am currently enrolled in CEC Intermediate (2026–2028). Following that, I plan to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Political Science, Economics, or History (2028–2031), and then appear for the UPSC Civil Services Examination in 2031, with the ambition of serving as an IAS or IPS officer.

My Passion for Technology

From artificial intelligence and robotics to programming, electronics, and web development — technology is a lens through which I explore the world. I believe every great innovation needs strong governance behind it, and every effective governance system can be improved through thoughtful use of technology.

My Vision

I want to serve as a civil servant who understands both technology and governance. I am especially interested in digital governance, accessibility, education systems, and how technology can improve public service delivery at scale.

Learning & Growth

I actively study programming, science, history, political theory, and emerging technologies. I approach learning as an ongoing process — each subject shapes how I understand both technology and the world around me.

Strength Beyond Challenges

Living with Cerebral Palsy has taught me patience, adaptability, and persistence. It continues to shape how I approach learning and problem-solving. I move forward steadily — learning, building, and growing one step at a time.
